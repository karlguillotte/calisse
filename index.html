Synopsis

Inspired from the "Pemberton Big F'N Loop", we have step up the challenge of this second edition. Be ready for lots of climbing and kilometres...

Let's meet on Saturday morning June 22nd at 8 at the Grizzly Plaza.

Here is the deal: everyone goes at their own pace: it is not a race, just a social event to push your limits. No entrance fee, no race marshals, no first aid, no prizes.

Invite people you think are crazy enough for this challenge.
