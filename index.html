Synopsis

Inspired from the "Pemberton Big F'N Loop", we have step up the challenge of this second edition. Be ready for lots of climbing and kilometres...

The plan is to meet Saturday morning at 8 at the Grizzly Plaza, you will get a map of the trails and some instructions, more details to come about an activity post-ride.

Everyone goes at their own pace, it is not a race, just a social event to push your limits. No entrance fee, no race marshals, no first aid, no prizes.

Invite people you think are crazy enough for this challenge.

Karl and David
